                            <p class="item-intro text-muted">Agriculture based application</p>
                            <h3>Description</h3>
<p>Coimbatore Institute of Technology invites all enthusiastic programmers around India for Hack2Harvest an online hackathon. Hackathon will make you put your thinking hats, get your hands dirty while making your idea shine. If you think you have what it takes to compete with the best in our country, the platform is yours to ideate, create and conquer. This hackathon is all about building an innovative API on our country’s back bone. There will be no restriction on the backend framework you use, but commonly used ones are preferred. This hackathon is open for both Professionals and student teams. Judging criteria will remain same for all teams.
You can participate in the hackathon individually or as a team of 2.
This hackathon will be conducted in 2 phases</p>
<h3>Theme</h3>   
<p>Agriculture based applications</p>
<p align='left'><b>Phase 1</b></p></br>
<p>Phase 1 is going to be an idea submission round where participants are supposed to submit their idea based on Agriculture. Idea submission will be open from 5th September till 15th September 2016. The expectation here is to submit a one page synopsis and a presentation around the idea. If you don’t submit your idea by the given date, you will not be considered for further rounds.</p>

<p align='left'><b>Phase 2</b><br>
<p>Top 10 participants will be shortlisted from phase 1 and will go to Phase 2 where Api presentations will happen. All top 10 participants are expected to present the working app at the judging panel in Coimbatore Institute of Technology. This will carry a weightage of 10% of the final score. Individuals / both team members are expected to be available to claim the prize in the prize distribution ceremony that will happen on 9th October at Coimbatore Institute Technology, Coimbatore.</p>
<h3>Rules</h3>

<p align='left'>•	This is a team participation hackathon. You can have at most 2 members in your team or you could participate individually.</br>

•	The hackathon will start at 20-09-16 and end at 23-09-2016, you can submit your hack during the allotted time only at the respective email id.</br>

•	It's an online and offline hackathon, 1st phase of event is going to be online and 2nd phase will be offline.</br>

•	Agriculture is  the theme of this hackathon, you must submit a hack on Agro based application. (e.g. cloud based rainfall detection, mobile controlled  well pumps, etc.)</br>

•	It is recommended that application that is going to be submitted should run on  any one of platforms like Android, iOS, Java Applet, Any web or mobile web.  </br>

•	You are expected to come up with new and innovative ideas, any idea that has been copied from somewhere will be disqualified.</br>

•	Your hack must be developed entirely during the Hackathon duration. You may use open source libraries and other freely available systems / services such as Google Maps, Facebook Connect, Twitter feeds etc.</br>

•	The intellectual property of your code belongs only to your team.</br>


•	Participant must bring their own laptops/mobiles for running  your application in the event.</p>
